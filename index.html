ABOUT ME

I am a guitarist, music producer, and mixing engineer from Monterrey, Mexico, now based in Berlin, Germany.

With over 10 years of experience in the music field and a deep love for crafting immersive soundscapes, I will help you craft music that inspires and leaves a lasting impression.

My approach is all about angular decisions and intuitive moves — capturing the spirit of each project, pushing boundaries, and always staying true to the artist's vision.

Driven by the idea of making music that resonates across borders, I focuses on every detail to create something both personal and universal, bringing a unique perspective — every detail counts.
